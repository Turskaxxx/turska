Some information

Massage therapy is basically manual manipulation of tissues. My style is mostly based on trigger points and understanding of fascia lines. I use techniques from multiple styles and use different methods according to the needs of the client. Massage is always a temporary solution and I use it mainly to make possible the foundations of enhancing the biomechanics.

Functional Patterns is a training method which focuses on natural movement patterns that are have been coded into us through evolution. We are built to walk, run and throw. Respecting these mechanics in training enables your body to start fixing itself over the course of proper exercise. Our sedentary way of life and unhealthy training methods have caused multiple problems which can be seen everywhere around, but fortunately, can in most cases be fixed.
